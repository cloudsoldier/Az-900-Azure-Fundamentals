

# Lab: Exploring Azure AI Services and Vision Studio

---

## **Objective**
Learn how to create an Azure AI Multi-Service Account and use Vision Studio to extract text from images.

---

## **Steps**

### **Step 1: Overview of Azure AI Services**
1. Azure provides a comprehensive suite of AI services, including:
   - **Vision**: Text extraction, object detection, and image analysis.
   - **Language**: Sentiment analysis, key phrase extraction, and translation.
   - **Speech**: Speech-to-text, text-to-speech, and speech translation.
   - **Decision-Making**: Anomaly detection and recommendations.
2. In this lab, you will use **Vision Studio** to extract text from images.

---

### **Step 2: Create an Azure AI Multi-Service Account**
1. Sign in to the **Azure Portal**.
2. Navigate to **Azure AI Services** in the menu.
3. Click on **Create Multi-Service Account**.
4. Configure the new account:
   - **Resource Group**: Select an existing resource group or create a new one.
   - **Region**: Choose **North Europe**.
   - **Pricing Tier**: Select **Standard**.
   - **Responsible AI Acknowledgment**: Check the box to agree to the terms.
5. Click **Review and Create** to validate the setup.
6. Once validated, click **Create** to deploy the resource.
7. After deployment, go to **All Resources** and verify the new AI resource.

---

### **Step 3: Use Vision Studio for Text Extraction**
1. Open **Vision Studio** from the Azure AI Services section.
2. Sign in with your Azure account credentials.
3. Select the newly created **AI Multi-Service Account** as the resource.
4. Confirm the selection and acknowledge the terms.
5. Choose one of the provided demo images in Vision Studio.
6. Use the **Extract Text** feature to process the image and observe the results.
7. Try additional demo images to explore the capabilities of Vision Studio further.

---

### **Step 4: Explore Other Azure AI Services**
1. Azure AI services offer various tools for additional use cases:
   - **Text Analytics**: Sentiment analysis, entity recognition, and language detection.
   - **Speech Services**: Convert audio to text, text to speech, and perform real-time translations.
   - **Language Understanding (LUIS)**: Build conversational bots and natural language processing models.
   - **Decision AI**: Build recommendation engines or detect anomalies.
2. Review these services to understand their applications in real-world scenarios.

---

### **Step 5: Clean Up Resources**
1. To avoid unnecessary charges, delete the resources you created:
   - Navigate to **All Resources** in the Azure Portal.
   - Select the **AI Multi-Service Account**.
   - Click **Delete** and confirm the deletion process.

---

## **Key Learnings**
- Azure AI services provide easy-to-use, prebuilt AI tools for various tasks.
- Vision Studio enables tasks like text extraction from images without requiring any coding knowledge.
- Responsible AI practices are essential when using Azure AI services.
